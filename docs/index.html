The idea

Three simple steps, on repeat.

There is no secret hack. The AI does the same thing a person does, just faster.

👀

It sees

The app reads the screen of your phone and finds the photos, name, and answers on each profile.

🤔

It decides

It thinks about the profile and chooses to like it or skip it, based on what you tell it you want.

👆

It taps

It taps the like or the skip button on the real phone, waits a moment, then moves to the next one.

Built to be safe

You stay in control.

One-tap stopClose the phone window and it stops the very same second. Nothing keeps running.
Locked-out appsIt cannot open your bank, wallet, photos, or messages. Those are blocked.
No passwordsIt uses the phone you are already signed in to. It never sees or saves any password.

Why this exists

This is a small project made only to show what AI agents can do. It is for learning and curiosity, not for making money. Automating a dating app can go against that app's rules (its Terms of Service), so please do not use this to break them. Use it carefully, on your own account, and at your own risk.
