Privacy

This website collects nothing. There are no accounts, no cookies, no trackers, and no analytics. We do not receive any of your data.

The tool itself runs on your own Mac. It controls your phone locally and does not send anything to us. If you choose a cloud AI model, the screen text and images it reads are sent to that AI provider so it can decide, and that data is handled under that provider's own privacy policy. If you choose a local model, nothing leaves your machine.

It never reads or stores passwords, and it is blocked from your wallet, banking, photos, mail, and messages.
