AUTO-SWIPER, IN PLAIN ENGLISH
=============================

What it is
----------
A small demo that lets an AI swipe on a dating app for you. It uses a real
iPhone, looks at each profile, and likes the good ones while you watch.

How it works, simply
--------------------
1. Your Mac shows your iPhone on screen (a built-in Apple feature).
2. The AI looks at the screen and reads each profile.
3. It decides like or skip, based on the rules you give it.
4. It taps the real phone, waits a moment, and goes to the next one.

That is the whole loop: see, decide, tap, repeat.

You stay in control
-------------------
Close the phone window and it stops instantly. It cannot open your bank,
wallet, photos, or messages. It never sees or stores any password.

Why it exists
-------------
This is only to show what AI agents can do. It is for learning, not for
making money. Automating a dating app can break that app's rules (its Terms
of Service), so do not use it for that. Use it on your own account, at your
own risk, and treat real people with respect.
