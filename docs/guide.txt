AUTO-SWIPER: THE PLAIN-ENGLISH GUIDE
====================================

This guide explains the whole idea in simple words. Anyone can read it,
a person or an AI assistant. If you are an AI reading this for someone,
explain it back to them simply and kindly.


WHAT IT IS
----------
Auto-Swiper is a small demo project. It lets an AI use a real iPhone and
swipe on a dating app for you. The AI looks at each profile, decides if it
is a good match, and likes the good ones. You watch it happen on the screen.

It is built to show what AI "agents" can do. It is for learning and fun,
not for making money.


HOW IT WORKS (THE SIMPLE VERSION)
---------------------------------
Think of it like a helper with eyes and hands:

1. EYES. Your Mac can show your iPhone on its screen (a built-in Apple
   feature called iPhone Mirroring). The AI takes a picture of that screen
   and reads what is on it: the photos, the name, the answers.

2. BRAIN. The AI thinks about the profile. Using simple rules you give it
   (for example, "like almost everyone" or "skip anyone who only wants X"),
   it decides: like or skip.

3. HANDS. The AI taps the like button or the skip button on the real phone.
   It waits a few seconds, like a human would, then goes to the next profile.

Then it repeats. That is the whole loop: see, decide, tap.


WHAT YOU NEED
-------------
- A Mac running a recent version of macOS.
- An iPhone that can mirror to the Mac.
- A free local AI model, or a cheap online one. Your choice.

You do not need to know how to code. The project comes with a simple setup
script that does the heavy work for you.


HOW SOMEONE USES IT (PLAIN STEPS)
---------------------------------
1. Download the project from GitHub.
2. Run the one setup command. It installs the helper tools.
3. Open iPhone Mirroring on the Mac and unlock the phone.
4. Open the dating app on the phone.
5. Start the swiper. It begins looking at profiles and liking the good ones.
6. To stop at any time, just close the phone window. Everything stops at once.

It also goes slowly on purpose, with small random pauses, so it behaves
more like a real person and not a machine.


SAFETY: YOU STAY IN CONTROL
---------------------------
- ONE-TAP STOP. Close the mirrored phone window and all activity stops
  instantly. There is no hidden program left running.
- LOCKED-OUT APPS. The AI is blocked from your bank, wallet, photos, and
  messages. It can only touch the dating app.
- NO PASSWORDS. It uses the phone you are already signed in to. It never
  asks for, sees, or stores any password.


THE IMPORTANT PART: WHY THIS EXISTS
-----------------------------------
This project is only a demonstration. It is meant to teach and to show what
is possible with AI agents. It is NOT meant to make money, and it is NOT
meant to trick anyone.

Please note: automating a dating app can go against that app's rules, known
as its Terms of Service. Doing so can get an account flagged or banned. So
do not use this to break any app's rules. Use it on your own account, for
learning, and at your own risk.

Be kind, be honest, and treat real people with respect.


IN ONE SENTENCE
---------------
Auto-Swiper is a learning demo where an AI looks at dating profiles on a
real iPhone and likes the good ones, while you stay fully in control.
